Founder & Editor

Premanjan Pradhan
EducatorPoetEditor Jharsuguda, OdishaOdia • English • Hindi

Born and raised in Jharsuguda, Odisha, Premanjan Pradhan has spent his career at the intersection of education and literary expression. As both a practising teacher and a published poet, he understands exactly what young writers need: not just a place to publish, but a place to be taken seriously.

Words & Visions grew from a simple belief — that a student in a small-town school in Odisha deserves the same platform as one from an elite city school. Creative expression should not depend on geography or institutional prestige.

"Every student who picks up a pen to write a poem is already a poet. My job is simply to give that poem a home."

Submission Rules

Submission Guidelines

Last updated: March 2025. Please read before submitting.

Word & Length Limits

  • Poems: Maximum 100 lines. No minimum.
  • Short Stories: 300–2,500 words.
  • Submissions outside these limits will be returned for revision.

Allowed Themes

We welcome poems and stories on: nature, love and loss, identity, patriotism, spirituality, family, humour, and coming-of-age experiences. We do not accept content that is violent, sexually explicit, hateful, politically inflammatory, or otherwise inappropriate for a student audience.

Originality & Plagiarism Policy

All submissions must be entirely the student's own original work. Copying, paraphrasing without attribution, or submitting another person's work as your own is plagiarism and will result in immediate rejection and forfeiture of the publishing contribution. We use AI-assisted plagiarism detection on all submissions.

AI Usage Policy

We do not accept poems or stories that were written, substantially drafted, or significantly edited by AI tools (including ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, or any other language model). We believe that the value of student publishing lies in genuine human expression. AI detection tools are used on every submission. AI-generated submissions are rejected without refund.

Editorial Rights

The editor reserves the right to make minor formatting corrections (line breaks, punctuation) without changing the meaning of the work. Substantive edits will always be communicated to the author for approval before publication. The editor may reject any submission for any reason, including but not limited to policy violations, quality concerns, or thematic unsuitability.

Publishing Contribution Policy

The publishing contribution (₹500 for poems, ₹1,000 for stories) covers editorial review, formatting, permanent hosting, and a Verified Original certificate. It is non-refundable once the editorial review process has begun. If the platform is unable to publish due to a system error on our end, a full refund will be issued within 7 working days.

Parent Consent

All students under the age of 18 must have parent or guardian consent before submitting. By checking the consent box on the submission form, you confirm that your parent or guardian is aware of and has approved this submission.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

What Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to process your submission: your name, class, school name, email address (and parent email for minors), poem/story text, and UPI Transaction ID. We do not collect phone numbers, home addresses, or financial details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your submission and process editorial review
  • To verify your UPI payment
  • To contact you about your submission status
  • To display your published work with attribution

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your information to third parties
  • We do not use your information for advertising
  • We do not share your parent's email with anyone
  • We do not store payment information beyond the Transaction ID

Student Data Protection

We are committed to protecting the privacy of minors. Only the author's name, class, and school are displayed publicly. Email addresses are never displayed. Parents may request removal of their child's data at any time by emailing us.

Copyright Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Author Retains Copyright

All students who publish on Words & Visions retain full copyright to their work. Publishing on this platform does not transfer ownership or exclusive rights to the platform.

Platform Licence

By submitting your work, you grant Words & Visions a non-exclusive, royalty-free licence to display, share, and archive your work on this platform and its associated social media channels with full attribution.

Removal Requests

Authors (or their parents, for minors) may request removal of their work at any time by emailing [email protected]. Removal is processed within 7 working days. Publishing contributions are non-refundable upon removal.

Unauthorised Use

Reproducing student work from this platform without the author's permission is a violation of copyright law. If you wish to reprint or reproduce a poem or story, please contact us to obtain the author's consent.

Content Moderation Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Editorial Review

Every submission is personally reviewed by the editor before publication. No content goes live without editorial approval.

Grounds for Rejection

  • Plagiarism or AI-generated content
  • Violent, sexually explicit, or hateful content
  • Content that identifies or endangers a real person
  • Politically inflammatory content
  • Content that violates a third party's copyright

Comment Moderation

Comments on published poems are moderated before appearing publicly. Offensive, harassing, or spam comments are deleted. Repeat offenders are blocked from commenting.

Reporting

To report a concern about published content or a comment, email [email protected] with the subject line "Content Report". We respond within 48 hours.
