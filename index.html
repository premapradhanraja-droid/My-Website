Mourning Son
to His Mother

Premanjan Pradhan — Elegy

Crying on second of year's month first
Nectar from her bosom quenched my thirst.
Sparkled in my tears an image cryin'
Whom later my mind thought divine.

Our Mission

A home for
every voice.

Words & Visions was built on one belief: every person who has felt something deeply enough to write it down deserves to be read. Not just published — truly read.

We are not a literary journal with gatekeepers. We are a curated space where original, verified writing finds a permanent, beautiful home — in any language, from any voice.

✍️
Open Submission
Any poet or writer may submit. No rejection letters, no prestige gatekeeping. The work speaks for itself.
🛡
Verified Original
AI-powered authenticity check on every submission. Protects your voice and every voice on this platform.
∞
Free Forever
No subscription. No premium tier. No monetisation of your words. Ever.
